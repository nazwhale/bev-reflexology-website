What is reflexology?

(noun) A system of massage used to relieve tension and treat illness, based on the theory that there are reflex points on the feet, hands, and head linked to every part of the body.

  • - Relaxation via foot massage.
  • - Reflief from blockages of energy fields, life-force, or Qi.
  • - Restoration of balance in the nervous system.
  • - Release of endorphins to reduce stress and pain.
  • - Rooted in ancient practices of China and Egypt.
